Our Mission

Driven by a profound dedication to social justice, the Lilavati Foundation was established to tackle vital issues like poverty, education, healthcare, and human rights for children and women. With a focus on empowering individuals and communities, the foundation aims to inspire positive change, raise awareness, and provide essential support where it's most needed. Through collaboration and advocacy, the foundation strives to create a lasting impact both locally and globally.

Goals

Addressing Core Issues:

The foundation focuses on alleviating poverty, improving access to education, providing healthcare, and advocating for child and women human rights. These areas are crucial for building a just and equitable society. Through these efforts, the foundation aims to create lasting change.

Empowerment and Advocacy:

By raising awareness and inspiring individuals to act, the foundation seeks to empower women to overcome their family challenges and improve their lives. Advocacy is a key component of this mission, aiming to influence policies and public opinion for positive change.

Community Support and Global Impact:

Recognizing and addressing the needs of urban and rural communities, the foundation provides essential services and support, stepping in where government agencies may fall short. This includes emergency relief, human trafficking, healthcare, education, and human rights advocacy.

Collaboration and Networking:

LILAWATI Foundation values collaboration with like-minded individuals, organizations, and institutions who would like to empower needy children and women. By building a network of supporters and partners, the foundation enhances its effectiveness and reach, ensuring that efforts are coordinated and impactful.
