Activities

Lilavati Foundation offers various support for needy child and women via various possible activities for improvement of health, education, and overall well-being. Here are some common activities and initiatives that can be undertaken to support child and women welfare:

Our Activites

  • Child Welfare Activities
  • Women Welfare Activities:
Healthcare Services: Support to provide access to vaccinations, regular health check-ups, and nutritional support, Offering mental health services and counselling.

Education: Providing scholarships, school supplies, and uniforms, Implementing early childhood education programs. Protection and Rights: Enforcing laws against child labour and child marriage, Conducting awareness campaigns on children's rights.

Recreation and Development: Organizing sports, arts, and cultural activities, Providing life skills and vocational training.

Nutrition and Recreation: Running feeding programs and distributing food packages, Educating families on proper nutrition and health practices.
Healthcare Services: Conducting health awareness programs and to provide counseling.

Education and Empowerment: Providing adult literacy programs and continuing education opportunities. Offering vocational training and skill development programs. Connecting the needy women for microfinance and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Protection and Rights: Legal assistance and support for women facing discrimination or violence. Campaigning for women's rights and gender equality.

Conducting workshops on topics like leadership, self-defense, and personal development. These activities aim to create a supportive environment that enables children and women to thrive, ensuring their rights are protected and their needs are met.
